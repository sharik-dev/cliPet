    Features

    Small, but very much alive

    Everything a desktop companion should do — and nothing that slows you down.

    In the menu bar

    No window, always there, never in the way. One click to tweak everything.

    Pixel animations

    It walks, runs, sits, sleeps, pounces and blinks. Hand-drawn, frame by frame.

    Chases your cursor

    Drop the yarn ball: it chases it across the screen and pounces on it.

    Clipboard manager

    A smart clipboard that detects hex colors, shows image thumbnails, tracks file paths, and saves photos to disk. Nothing slips through.

    Custom skins

    Change the cat's coat and style. Several skins included, more on the way.

    Sprite editor

    Draw your own pixel animations right inside the app. Your cat, your rules.

    Captures everything

    Text, images, photos, PDFs, files, hex colors — every copy is saved with a rich preview. Your full clipboard history, searchable in one panel.

    100% native

    Swift & AppKit. No Electron, under 50 MB of RAM. Fast and discreet.

    Privacy-first

    No cloud, no account, no personal data. Your clipboard never leaves your Mac — only anonymous usage stats.
